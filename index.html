PAF COLLEGE LOWER TOPA MURREE HILLS

Overview of PAF College Lower Topa

PAF College Lower Topa, established in 1952, is an all-boys military boarding school affiliated with the Pakistan Air Force (PAF). Located on a hilltop near Murree, Pakistan, the college's motto is "I Will Dare". The current principal is Air Vice Marshal Muhammad Khalid Dabir (Retd). The mission of the college is to groom future leaders and officers for the PAF, particularly for General Duty (Pilot) roles.

Topian Honor Code

We, the pre-cadets of PAF College Lower Topa, Murree, and we shall never cheat, steal or lie, nor tolerate among us anyone who does so.

College Mission

To provide quality education and training to students, enabling them to become well-rounded individuals who can serve Pakistan with pride and distinction.

Facilities

The college provides free lodging, mess, and medical care for the cadets. It has academic blocks, labs, a library, an auditorium, a mosque, a gymnasium, and sports facilities. Extracurricular activities include national day celebrations (Independence Day, Air Force Day), leadership training, and discipline programs.

Admission Process for 2025

Eligibility: Age: 11.5–13.5 years by January 1, 2025. Must pass Class 7 by April 2025 (Cambridge system students eligible). Male citizens of Pakistan or Azad Jammu & Kashmir. No spectacle wearer is eligible.

Key Dates: Online Registration: September 30–October 23, 2024. Classes Start: May 2025.

Selection Process: Written Test covers English, Urdu, Mathematics, and General Science (aligned with FBISE Class VII syllabus). Medical Exam & Interview conducted at centers like Rawalpindi, Karachi, Lahore, etc. Final Merit List based on test scores, medical fitness, and interview performance.

Fee Structure: Free education, boarding, uniforms, and medical facilities. Examination fee: Rs. 500–2,500 (varies by source).
